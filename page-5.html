Data engineers focus on building and maintaining the infrastructure required for efficient data collection, storage, and processing. They design and develop data pipelines, database systems, and ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes. Data engineers ensure data quality, availability, and reliability for data scientists and analysts to perform their work effectively.

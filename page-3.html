A business analyst plays a crucial role in bridging the gap between data analysis and business
decision-making. They gather and analyze data to identify trends, patterns, and insights that can drive strategic decision-making within an organization. Business analysts collaborate with stakeholders, understand business requirements, and provide recommendations based on data analysis to optimize business processes and improve performance.

Preferred skills: Data visualization, business intelligence, SQL
Role and Responsibilities: Improve business process. Serve as an intermediary between business and IT requirements. Data visualization tools. Business Intelligence Understanding. Data Modelling
